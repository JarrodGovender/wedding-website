Gentlemen

• The Standard: High-fashion evening tailoring. Sharp black suits, dark luxury dinner jackets, or premium classic tuxedos.

Ladies

• The Standard: High-fashion high-end aesthetic. Elegant floor-length formal gowns or avant-garde high-fashion cocktail wear.

• Colors: Rich colors, jewel tones, or solid black are highly encouraged.

• Strict Ban: Gowns or dresses featuring white, cream, ivory, beige, or light vanilla shades are strictly prohibited.
